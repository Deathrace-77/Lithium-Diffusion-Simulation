Simulating Lithium Diffusion

Understanding Nano vs Micro Particle Performance

TKS Focus Project

Project Overview

This interactive simulation demonstrates how particle size dramatically affects lithium-ion diffusion rates in battery electrodes. By modeling the relationship between particle radius and charging time, we can visualize why nanotechnology is revolutionizing battery performance.

Key Insight: Diffusion time scales with radius squared (t ∝ L²). Reducing particle size from micrometers to nanometers can reduce charging times by factors of thousands.
